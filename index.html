About Project

Why LinkShield AI?

LinkShield AI is an intelligent cybersecurity solution designed to protect everyday internet users from the growing threat of phishing attacks. Using advanced machine learning algorithms, our system analyzes URLs in real-time to identify potential threats before they can harm you.

Phishing attacks are becoming increasingly sophisticated, with millions of users falling victim each year. Our mission is to make the internet safer by providing an accessible, easy-to-use tool that anyone can use to verify suspicious links.
